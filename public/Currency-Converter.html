❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Currency?
Currency is a medium of exchange, a unit of account, and a store of value used for transactions between parties.
What is Currency Fluctuation?
Currency fluctuation refers to the changes in the value of one currency in relation to another due to market forces like supply and demand.
What is Exchange Rate?
The exchange rate is the rate at which one currency can be exchanged for another, determining how much of one currency you get for another.
What is Forex Market?
The Forex (Foreign Exchange) market is a global decentralized market for trading currencies, the largest and most liquid market in the world.
What is Currency Conversion?
Currency conversion is the process of exchanging one currency for another, typically at an agreed-upon exchange rate.
